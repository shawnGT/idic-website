Welcome to the IDIC • A Star Trek Online Fleet

Welcome Our Newest Member!

This last week we recruited a new member during our regularly scheduled Fleet Battles. Please welcome Admiral Spunk Jeans! He comes to us from the "Survivors of Q" and looks to join us in the upcomming colonization missions in the Delta Quadrant. He is a specialist of Science and loves PVP. He would like to know if you want to run some battles with him feel free to drop him a message!

Check Your Inventory!

After the last patch a few members have been reporting that their inventory is missing some items. The good news is that Cryptic seems to be on it and will help you recover anything you might be missing. We aren't saying you should tell them that you were missing items you didn't have but maybe you lost some Zen?

Fleet Week!

From October 10th until the 15th Cryptic and Modiphius(the company behind the Star Trek TTRPG) will be teaming up for Fleet Week! All week there will be live stream events with different designers who work on the various games and it seems they will be focusing a lot on ship design. There might be new cool announcements and chances to win prizes. If you are at all interested check out more information on the link below!

Small Changes to Our Weekly Schedule

I hope this will work better for the majority of us. I know some UK folks really needed this because Wednesday did not work for them. If anybody has any qestions or suggestions please let me know!
